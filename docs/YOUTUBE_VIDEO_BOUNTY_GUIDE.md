# YouTube Video Bounty Guide

This guide explains how to create and submit a YouTube video about **RustChain** and **BoTTube** to earn the [bounty #14089](https://github.com/Scottcjn/rustchain-bounties/issues/14089).

## 🎯 Requirements

- **Real channel**: Your YouTube channel must have **100+ subscribers** (no zero-view uploads).
- **Original content**: Do not reupload existing videos. Create something unique.
- **Mention these projects**:
  - [RustChain](https://rustchain.org) – Proof-of-Physical-AI / antiquity mining (vintage hardware earns more)
  - [BoTTube](https://bottube.ai) – AI agent video platform (119+ agents)
  - (Optional but recommended) [Beacon Atlas](https://github.com/Scottcjn/BeaconAtlas), [Xonotic RustChain Arena](https://github.com/Scottcjn/Xonotic-RustChain-Arena), [clawrtc miner](https://github.com/Scottcjn/clawrtc)
- **Include links**: In the video description, link to the repos and the bounty issue.
- **Length**: At least 5 minutes (shorter videos may be accepted if high quality).

## 📦 Reward

**50–200 RTC** depending on quality, audience engagement, and depth of coverage.
- Base reward for a solid overview: **50 RTC**
- +50 RTC for including a live demo (mining with vintage hardware or using BoTTube)
- +50 RTC for high production value (editing, audio, captions)
- +50 RTC for exceptional reach (views > 500 in first week)

## 📝 How to Submit

1. Publish your video on YouTube.
2. Comment on [issue #14089](https://github.com/Scottcjn/rustchain-bounties/issues/14089) with:
   - Your video link
   - Your wallet address
   - A short description of what you covered
3. Wait for review. Payout is sent within 7 days after verification.

## 💡 Tips

- Target a general tech/blockchain audience – explain why “Proof-of-Antiquity” is unique.
- Show real hardware (old laptop, Raspberry Pi, PowerPC) mining RustChain.
- Mention the bounty program to attract other contributors.
- Use tags: `#RustChain #BoTTube #ProofOfAntiquity #CryptoMining`

## ✅ Checklist

- [ ] Channel has 100+ subscribers
- [ ] Video is at least 5 minutes
- [ ] Mentions RustChain and BoTTube with links
- [ ] Description includes repo/issue links
- [ ] Video is original (not reused content)
- [ ] Commented on the issue with wallet address
